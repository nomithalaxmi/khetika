💧 Irrigation Tip

Water crops in the early morning (5–8 AM) or evening (5–7 PM) to minimize evaporation and prevent leaf burn.

🌱 Soil Health Tip

Add organic matter (compost/green manure) every season. It improves soil structure, water retention, and nutrient availability.

🐛 Pest Prevention

Scout your field every 3–4 days. Early detection of pests prevents 80% of crop damage before it becomes severe.
